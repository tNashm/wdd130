About Us

Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond. We were started by the grandfather of the current owner and operator. Family is very important to us, so we try to curate an experience that will be fun for everyone

Rapid River Rafters offers rafting on the Colorodo and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs.
